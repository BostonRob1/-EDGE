/ HOW TO EXPORT /

Click a graphic. Screenshot the centered card. Post.

  1. Open any tile's OPEN ↗ button — the graphic renders at its native size (e.g. 1080×1080) centered on a checkerboard backdrop.
  2. Window-screenshot just the graphic (Cmd-Shift-4-Space on Mac → click the card). The corner "EXPORT AT…" badge sits outside the canvas so it won't show in the crop.
  3. Alternatively use Chrome devtools → device mode at the listed dimension and capture full page.
  4. Where you see __________ or dashed slots — fill the contract address / market name / quote before posting.
