# "EDGE OF THE WEEK" — WEEKLY RECAP FORMAT

**Cadence:** Every Sunday, 8pm ET.
**Format:** 16:9 (1920×1080) primary, 1:1 + 9:16 cutdowns
**Total runtime:** 3.0–5.0 minutes (target 4:00)
**Distribution:** YouTube long-form, X native video, Spotify (audio-only podcast version), TikTok cutdowns of each segment
**Style:** Bloomberg-meets-podcast. Stock-tickerlike chrome. Single host or co-hosts. The data does the talking.

---

## SHOW BIBLE

**Pitch in one sentence:** "Every Sunday, $EDGE shows you the three biggest stories in prediction markets you should've been watching."

**Three fixed segments, in order, every week:**
1. **WHALE OF THE WEEK** — the biggest single whale position taken
2. **DIVERGENCE OF THE WEEK** — the market with the widest money-vs-mouth gap
3. **MOVE OF THE WEEK** — the market that moved the most in dollars

Plus a **COLD OPEN** before segment 1 and a **CLOSE** after segment 3.

- **End cards:** Standard $EDGE end card always

---

## WRITING RULES (from brand voice doc)

1. Lead with the dollar figure.
2. Wallet addresses always shortened (`0xA3F2...8C2D`).
3. Never editorialize who's "smart" or "dumb." Show the data, let the viewer judge.
4. Never name retail traders without consent. Whales are public wallets — fair game.
5. Always concede uncertainty when the data is ambiguous. ("Could be a hedge — we can't tell.")
6. End every episode with the brand tagline: "Watch the action. Before you bet."
