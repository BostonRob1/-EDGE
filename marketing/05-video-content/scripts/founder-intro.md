## DIRECTOR NOTES

- **No faces, no exceptions.** If a shot would reveal identity, replace with screen content, mono text, or brand graphics. The anonymity is non-negotiable.
- **Pacing:** Slow. Voices that rush sound like they're hiding something. Voices that don't rush — even anonymous ones — sound certain.
- **Voice direction:** Dry, declarative, slightly clinical. NOT a hype voice. Reference: Bloomberg terminal explainer, Apple keynote narration, mid-2010s indie documentary voiceover. Not crypto-bro, not founder-podcast.
- **No B-roll filler:** Every screen cutaway shows the actual product or the actual on-chain data. No stock footage of charts or trading floors.
- **Color:** Color grade the screen captures cool — low saturation, slight teal in shadows — to match the void-and-lime brand.
- **Sound:** Record VO in a treated room. Cut plosives in post. Lay a low ambient pad underneath at -22LU.
- **Length:** If a beat runs long, cut Shot 06 (the demo) — the demo lives in other deliverables.
- **Identity safety review:** Before publishing, watch back at 0.5x with no audio. If any frame could be traced to a specific person (a desk arrangement, a window view, a piece of jewelry, a tattoo, a stylized mouse pointer, a custom IDE theme), reshoot or mask.
