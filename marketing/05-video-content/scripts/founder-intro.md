## DIRECTOR NOTES

- **Pacing:** Slow. Founders who rush sound like they're hiding something.
- **Eye line:** Both founders should look slightly off-lens, not directly at camera. Less "selling," more "explaining."
- **No B-roll filler:** Every screen cutaway shows the actual product. No stock footage of charts or trading floors.
- **Color:** Color grade the founder footage cool — low saturation, slight teal in shadows — to match the void-and-lime brand.
- **Sound:** Lavalier mics on both. Record room tone. Cut out plosives in post.
- **Length:** If a beat runs long, cut Shot 06 (the demo) — the demo lives in other deliverables.
