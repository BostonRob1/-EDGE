# 30-SECOND KOL ENDORSEMENT VIDEO — TEMPLATE

**Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080×1920) primary, 1:1 reframe for X
**Total runtime:** 30.0s
**Use:** Send to KOLs (key opinion leaders) — they record themselves over our B-roll, or we cut their existing clip into our template.
**Style guard:** Their voice, our frame. The brand is the chrome around the human.

---

## POSITIONING — WHO WE PARTNER WITH

**Tier A (priority):** Prediction-market natives — Polymarket Pro traders, Kalshi power users, "smart money" Twitter accounts with verified track records.
**Tier B:** Crypto KOLs with credibility (no shill farms). Account age >2yr, follower:engagement >2%.
**Tier C:** Macro / political-betting personalities (e.g. election cycle voices).

**Avoid:** Pump-and-dump shills, anon airdrop farmers, "100x gem" accounts, anyone with >5 paid promos in the last 30 days.

**Talking points (give KOL 3, they pick 1):**
1. The whale-alert beat — "I caught a $2M Trump-2028 move 4 minutes before it hit Twitter."
2. The divergence beat — "I bet against the crowd because $EDGE showed me the sentiment-price gap."
3. The terminal beat — "I had 4 tabs open. Now I have one screen."

## TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

### SHOT 01 — 0.0s–2.0s · INTRO FRAME

**VISUAL:** KOL face-cam (or static portrait) lower-third. Top of screen: $EDGE mark + their @handle in mono 14px lime. Bottom of screen: their lower-third name plate.

**KOL SAYS:** "Yo, [KOL name] here."

**CAPTION:** Burn-in their handle + a single line of credential — e.g. `@WHALECHASER · POLYMARKET TOP 100 PnL`.

---

### SHOT 02 — 2.0s–18.0s · THE ENDORSEMENT (THEIR BEAT)

**VISUAL:** Hold on KOL with their B-roll cutaway at 6s, 12s. Each cutaway is screen-record of $EDGE — the panel they're referencing.

**KOL SAYS:** Free-form, picking one of the three talking points. 16 seconds of actual content.

**CAPTION:** Burn-in their words verbatim (subtitle).

**B-ROLL REQUIRED (we supply):**
- 5s loop of whale alerts feed printing
- 5s loop of divergence radar with magenta outlier pulsing
- 5s loop of markets grid scrolling
- 5s loop of $EDGE mark / wordmark draw

KOL or editor picks two cutaways from this pack based on the beat they chose.

---

### SHOT 03 — 18.0s–24.0s · THE PROOF

**VISUAL:** Cut to full-bleed screen recording of the exact feature the KOL just described. No KOL on screen — the product speaks. Magenta or lime accent depending on feature.

**KOL SAYS:** (voiceover continues if natural, else silence)

**CAPTION:** `[FEATURE NAME] · $EDGE` — mono 20px lime, top-right.

---

### SHOT 04 — 24.0s–28.0s · CTA

**VISUAL:** KOL face-cam back. Their hand gesture or "look down" cue. Caption block prints in to their left.

**KOL SAYS:** "Edge terminal dot xyz. Link in bio. Go look."

**CAPTION:** Three lines stacked, Anton 36px:
- `EDGETERMINAL.XYZ`
- `$EDGE ON PUMP.FUN`
- `CA: [CONTRACT_ADDRESS]`

---

### SHOT 05 — 28.0s–30.0s · END CARD

**VISUAL:** Standard $EDGE end card (matches all other video deliverables) — mark + wordmark + `thepolyedge.com`. KOL handle in corner: `IN PARTNERSHIP WITH @[handle]` mono 11px muted.

**KOL SAYS:** Silent.

**CAPTION:** None.

## CTA PLACEMENT RULES

- **Never** put CTA in first 5s — the algorithm penalizes early outbound intent.
- **Always** repeat the URL once spoken + once burned-in.
- **Disclosure:** Add `#ad` or `#partner` at end of caption per FTC + each platform's policy. Don't try to hide it — credible voices disclose.
