## VOICE NOTES

- **Anton headlines** in display use; bios are body voice (DM Sans cadence — clauses, periods, breath).
- Lead with a noun, not a verb. ("$EDGE is..." / "The terminal for...") Action verbs work in headlines, not bios.
- The phrase "Watch the action. Before you bet." appears in every tier that has room. Earn the right to drop it only in tier 1 + 6B.
- "Bloomberg" appears once in tier 3 — never again. We are *like* Bloomberg; we don't keep saying so.
- Numbers in mono context only ("$1B in cumulative volume"). Don't sprinkle figures into prose for decoration.
- Never abbreviate to "PM" for prediction markets — too easy to misread. Spell it out.
