Boilerplate

09.02 · Approved Copy

Short — 1 sentence

$EDGE is a real-time intelligence terminal for prediction markets — Polymarket, Kalshi, whale flows, and social sentiment in one screen.

Medium — 1 paragraph

$EDGE is a Bloomberg-terminal-grade aggregator for prediction markets. The product pulls live data from Polymarket and Kalshi, tracks the largest wallets across both venues, and overlays public sentiment from X and Reddit. The proprietary divergence score identifies markets where on-chain money and public sentiment disagree — surfacing the edge before it closes. The $EDGE token (Solana) gates premium signal access; the underlying product is free and requires no signup.

Long — 3 paragraphs

$EDGE is the first intelligence layer for prediction markets. Prediction markets are projected to clear $15B+ in 2026 volume across Polymarket and Kalshi, yet professional operators still bounce across six browser tabs to see where capital is moving, what wallets are taking the other side, and what the public is saying about the same event. $EDGE collapses that workflow into one screen.

The product is built around three primitives: money (live order flow and whale wallet tracking across Polymarket's Gamma + CLOB and Kalshi's API), mouth (sentiment volume scraped from X, Reddit, and YouTube comments on the same questions), and edge (a proprietary divergence score where capital and sentiment disagree — historically the most reliable signal of mispricing). Founded by Robert and Tye in 2026, $EDGE is bootstrapped and ships on Vercel.

The $EDGE token (Solana, fairlaunch via pump.fun, 1B supply) gates premium product tiers — whale alerts, the divergence radar, raw API access, and a private insider channel. Liquidity is locked for 12 months, team allocation is 2% (vested over 24 months with a 6-month cliff), and treasury sits in a multi-sig on Squads.so. The terminal itself is and will remain free to use without a wallet connection.
