## The product, in one sentence

> $EDGE shows you where the money is, where the money is mispriced across venues, and what the public is saying about the same event — in real time, in one screen.

## The product, in one paragraph

$EDGE aggregates live market data from Polymarket and Kalshi, tracks the largest wallets active on each venue, and overlays public sentiment from X and Reddit on the same questions. A live **Arbitrage Radar** scans for same-event price discrepancies between the two venues and surfaces fee-adjusted opportunities. A proprietary **Divergence Score** identifies markets where on-chain capital and public sentiment disagree — historically the most reliable signal of mispricing. The underlying terminal is free and requires no signup. The $EDGE token gates premium signal access.

---

## The six product pillars

1. **Markets hub** — Polymarket + Kalshi in one screen. No-signup browsing.
2. **Whale wallet tracking** — Live firehose of large positions across both venues. Leaderboard + drilldown.
3. **Social signal aggregator** — Market-relevant chatter from X, Reddit, and public RSS pulled per-question.
4. **Divergence Radar** — Proprietary score where money and sentiment disagree, surfacing mispricings before the herd notices.
5. **Arbitrage Radar (NEW)** — Live cross-platform scanner over Polymarket ↔ Kalshi. Same event, different prices, fee-adjusted edges ranked HIGH / MEDIUM / VERIFY. The free-money pillar.
6. **Track record** — Append-only public ledger of every divergence call the system makes. Receipts by design.
