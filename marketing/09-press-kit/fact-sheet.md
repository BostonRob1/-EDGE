## The product, in one sentence

> $EDGE shows you where the money is, what the mouth is saying, and the gap between them — in real time, in one screen.

## The product, in one paragraph

$EDGE aggregates live market data from Polymarket and Kalshi, tracks the largest wallets active on each venue, and overlays public sentiment from X and Reddit on the same questions. Its proprietary **divergence score** identifies markets where on-chain capital and public sentiment disagree — historically the most reliable signal of mispricing. The underlying terminal is free and requires no signup. The $EDGE token gates premium signal access.

---

## The five product pillars

1. **Markets hub** — Polymarket + Kalshi in one screen. No-signup browsing.
2. **Whale wallet tracking** — Live firehose of large positions across both venues. Leaderboard + drilldown.
3. **Social signal aggregator** — Market-relevant chatter from X, Reddit, and public RSS pulled per-question.
4. **Proprietary picker algorithm** — Synthesizes whales + chatter + price velocity + sentiment into per-market picks. Published to a public track record.
5. **Social layer** — Profiles, follow, post picks, share alpha. The trader's social graph for prediction markets.
