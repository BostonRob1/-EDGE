# $EDGE — Embargo Policy

We honor embargoes. We also expect them honored back.

---

## Standard terms

- **Embargo length**: 24–72 hours from time of materials delivery, agreed in writing.
- **Materials delivered**: full fact sheet, founder availability window, asset ZIP, embargoed price/launch data.
- **Coverage publication**: any time after embargo lift (UTC).
- **Violations**: breaking embargo by even 5 minutes disqualifies the outlet from future early access.

## What we share under embargo

- Token launch date and time
- Final tokenomics (after any pre-launch tweaks)
- Strategic partners (with their explicit permission)
- Funding announcements (when applicable)
- Major product milestones
- Quotes from an Trader / an Trader (attributable, on the record)

## What we do not share under embargo

- Source code beyond what is public on GitHub
- User data of any kind
- Wallet addresses of whale subjects (these are public on-chain but we don't aggregate-and-deliver them)
- Pricing or volume data we don't yet have publication rights to

## How to request

Email **cashbridgehomes@gmail.com** with:
1. Outlet name and your byline link
2. Story angle (one paragraph)
3. Embargo length you need
4. Hard publication date/time (UTC)
5. Whether you need a founder Q&A

We'll respond within 24 hours, almost always with yes.

## What gets you Tier-1 access

We define Tier-1 outlets as those whose coverage moves traders. We give Tier-1 outlets first look, longer embargoes, and direct founder access. Tier-1 self-defines by readership — not by ad spend. If you write for traders, partners, or traders in the prediction-markets, fintech, or crypto-infrastructure space, you qualify.
