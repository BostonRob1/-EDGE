The Manifesto · Twelve theses

Receipts over résumés.

This is how we work and why. Twelve theses the Operators wrote before the first line of $EDGE got committed. They haven't changed since.

— Begin —
01

Money is the only honest signal.

Tweets lie. Pundits lie. Pollsters lie. Wallets don't. When a trader puts $328K on the line, that bet is more honest than a thousand op-eds. $EDGE starts from the money because the money is where the truth lives.

02

But the mouth moves first.

Capital is informed. Chatter is fast. Sometimes the loudest room is the leading indicator; sometimes it's a noise trap. $EDGE surfaces the divergence — not the consensus. The edge is the gap.

03

Anonymous by design.

Your last trade should matter more than your last name. We chose to stay anonymous so the work has to carry the credibility. No headshots. No LinkedIns. No biographical claims. Voice-only on Spaces. Code on GitHub. Tokens on-chain.

04

Public by code.

Anonymous doesn't mean opaque. Every API endpoint, every algorithm, every pricing decision is in the repo. If you can read JavaScript, you can audit $EDGE. Don't trust us — verify the commit.

05

The product comes first.

We didn't write a whitepaper before we wrote the code. The Dashboard works. The Divergence Radar fires real calls. The Arb scanner pulls real spreads. You can use $EDGE today, for free, without a wallet. The token only gates the deeper layers.

06

The token has a job.

$EDGE is not a meme dressed as utility. It unlocks specific, named features — whale alerts, divergence radar, raw API. Each tier maps to a concrete product surface. If we ever can't point to what your tokens do, we have failed.

07

Revenue burns the supply.

50% of every affiliate dollar buys $EDGE on the open market and burns it on-chain. The more the product is used, the rarer the token gets. Not a scheduled burn. Not a vibes-based burn. A revenue-driven flywheel that compounds in public.

08

Every call is timestamped.

When the algorithm surfaces a divergence — that call hits an append-only on-chain log before the market resolves. We can't edit it. We can't backdate it. Win or lose, the receipts compound.

09

No presale. No VCs. No early bag.

Fair launch on pump.fun. Zero team allocation. Both Operators buy on the curve like everybody else. If we hold, it's because we bought it — same as you. The treasury is a Squads multi-sig. Movements are public.

10

Free terminal, forever.

The base product never gets paywalled. Dashboard, Edge, Arb, Buzz, Whales, Radar — all stay free. Token utility unlocks deeper layers, never the floor. The free tier is the funnel, not the bait.

11

The community is the edge.

We're not the smartest traders in prediction markets. The smartest traders are. We're building the room where they gather — and routing the alpha they generate back through the token. Day-Zero holders steer the algorithm. Governance is the long game.

12

Ship every week.

Eleven product surfaces in the first month. Receipts compound when you ship in public. Velocity is the only moat that scales with team size — and we're two. So we move.

— Signed —
