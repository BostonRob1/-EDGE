§ 01 — METHODOLOGY

The signal lives between the two.

Every prediction market is a two-sided machine. On one side, capital at risk — visible, on-chain, brutally honest. On the other, narrative — what people are saying on X, in news, on Reddit, in research notes. Most of the time they agree. When they don't, that is the edge.
